Our Story

Merlins Group was born from a simple observation: businesses were drowning in manual processes that technology could easily automate. Founded by Prabjot Kandola and Santokh Singh, two veterans of enterprise software, we set out to change how companies operate.

What started as a consultancy quickly evolved into a technology powerhouse. We don't just advise on automation – we build it, deploy it, and ensure it transforms your business.

The Easy2Recruit portal represents everything we stand for: taking a complex, manual, time-consuming process and making it completely automated, intelligent, and effortless.

Our Mission

To eliminate manual work from business processes, allowing companies to focus on growth, innovation, and what truly matters – their customers and their people.
